Social Members Status Improvement Co-operative (SMSIC) has founded by the male and female from Nkwaki village. Our office is located in LC I Kyabayanja village, Kisaru Parish, Kabwoya Sub-County, Kikuube District. Our mission is to mitigate challenges affecting our community and our families. The Well-being of Co-operative members and their families through farming and rearing is our Vision. The objectives of Social Members Status Improvement Co-operative (SMSIC) is to eradicate poverty through sustainable solutions to drought, farming and rearing.

membership

All residence og Kabwoya Sub-country are considers as members of the group. The membership of the Co-operative shall be open to a person who identify with and supports the existence, Mission and Objectives and principals of group and who apply to register as a member and is approved by the members. Here below are the required criteria to get a membership
• Registration fee is 50,000 UGX.
• Member of the Co-operative should have religion
• To respect the laws and values of our community(Uganda)
• Self-respect
• Membership is voluntary
• A person willing and able to participate regularly in the Co-operative activities.
• A person known by majority of members of the Co-operative as trustworthy,
